How to get links

1. Upload an image to get a .patt file here.

2. Upload your .patt and .gltf (Don't forget the bin file) to github.

3. Open the uploaded file on github.

4. Choose "Raw" at the top of the file on github.

5. Copy the url directly in your browser.

Our Goal

We aim to bring Augmented Reality to everyone who has access to the internet. We will allow anyone to easily upload and download images and 3D models to our website. From there, they can use the AR to present and easily manuever their 3D models for areas such as Education, Advertisement, Communication and much more. You can try out the Augmented Reality right here!


We need to embark on a human revolution. A revolution where our reality is not replaced by drones but augmented by technology to do better - Vishal Sikka.
